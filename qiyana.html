QIYANA

EMPRESS OF THE ELEMENTS

Role: Assassin

Difficulty: Hard

Passive: Royal Privilege

Qiyana's basic attacks and basic abilities deal 15 − 83 (based on level) (+ 55% bonus AD) (+ 30% AP) additional physical damage to affected enemies.

This effect cannot occur on the same target more than once every few seconds.

Gathering an Element with Terrashape resets Royal Privilege's cooldown on all targets that had Royal Privilege applied to them with a different Element or with no Element.

Q: Edge of Ixtal

Qiyana slashes forward in the target direction, dealing physical damage to enemies in a line, reduced to 75% damage against targets beyond the first. Edge of Ixtal deals 150% damage against monsters.

Upon gathering an element with Terrashape Terrashape, Edge of Ixtal is upgraded to Elemental Wrath and its cooldown is reset.

If cast during Audacity's dash towards an enemy champion and the target is within 150 units of Qiyana at the end of the dash, Edge of Ixtal will autonomously aim at the target.

W: Terrashape

PASSIVE: While holding an Element, Qiyana gains Attack speed icon.png bonus attack speed, Range icon.png 25 bonus attack range and bonus magic damage on her basic attacks On-hit icon.png on-hit, Audacity Audacity and the Elemental Wrath Elemental Wrath cast that discharges it. While out-of-combat and moving near the Element currently being held, she gains Movement speed icon.png bonus movement speed.

ACTIVE: Qiyana Dash.png dashes up to 300 units towards the target location while also gathering an Element of the nearest Qiyana Terrashape Brush.png BRUSH, Qiyana Terrashape River.png RIVER or Qiyana Terrashape Rock.png TERRAIN she targeted, enchanting her blade. A target Element is required to cast this ability. Once Terrashape has been learned, Qiyana is automatically given the Qiyana Terrashape Rock.png TERRAIN element upon respawning. Qiyana can cast any of her abilities during Terrashape.

E: Audacity

ACTIVE: Qiyana Dash.png dashes a fixed distance towards the target enemy, dealing them physical damage if they are still in range upon arrival.

Qiyana can cast any of her abilities during Audacity.

R: Supreme Display of Talent

ACTIVE: Qiyana sends a windblast in the target direction that Airborne icon.png knocks back all enemies within its path and stops upon hitting terrain.

The windblast then creates an erupting shockwave across any RIVER or BRUSH it passes through, as well as around the borders of terrain it dealing physical damage to all enemies struck, with the percentage damage capped against Monster icon.png monsters, Stun icon.png stunning them for 0.5 − 1 (based on proximity) seconds, and granting Sight icon.png sight of the area along its path each for 0.75 seconds.
